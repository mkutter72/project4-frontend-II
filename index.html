Community Cares

Creating a community surrounding a person in need
Welcome to Community Cares. Community Cares helps caretakers get organized and improve communication when caring for an individual. Community Cares provides the following functionality:

Online Calendar

Coordinate your schedules with our OnLine Calendar. See a complete list of visit and service dates that are scheduled by members of the community.

Message Boards

Each boards consist of a separate conversation based on topic. See a history of the message board and if multiple members are on a message board it operates like a chat room.

Community Wall

Post greetings and caring thoughts for the center of the community. Pictures can be posted as well.
